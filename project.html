A Visualization that shows the top richest Football (soccer) clubs in the English Premier league, their positions for the past 8 years and Player's Annual salary

There are twenty teams in the English (Barclays) Premier League and each team play against each other twice
within the space of nine months, after which the club with the highest points wins the league
To view the salaries hover over the circles
